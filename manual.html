How To Use the Title Generator Page

Enter the title of your story.
Upload your story's cover image
Click on NEXT

You will see a card displaying your Title and cover image.
If something went wrong, you will see the default cover image (the website logo).
In that case, simply Go Back and try again!
Otherwise, when you're done admiring your work, you can either:

  1. Go Back to the "Create Your Own Title" page and design a new title
  2. Print a sample version of what your title page may look like
  3. Start writing the story!
The page gives you options for all three of those possibilities by clicking on the relevant buttons.

How To Use the Main Character Generator Page

Enter the name of your main character.
Upload your character's profile image (Click on Choose File and navigate through your file system).
Enter the character's email address, height (in feet and then inches), backstory, birthday, most annoying habit, dominant personality trait, and catch-phrase.
You can leave out any of these last three characteristics if you so desire.
Click on NEXT
You will see a card displaying all the information about your character and the profile image.
If something went wrong, you will see the default profile image (the RN profile image).
In that case, simply Go Back and try again!
Otherwise, when you're done admiring your work, you can either:

  1. Go Back to the "Create Your Own Main Character" page and design a new main character profile
  2. Print out the characterization of your main character
  3. Start writing the story!
The page gives you options for all three of those possibilities by clicking on the relevant buttons.
