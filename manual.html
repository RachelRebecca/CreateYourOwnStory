How To Use the Title Generator Page

Enter the title of your story.
Upload your story's cover image
Click on NEXT

You will see a card displaying your Title and cover image.
If something went wrong, you will see the default cover image (the website logo).
NOTE: Something is likely to go wrong if you are not connected to a server or to localhost.
In that case, simply Go Back and try again!
Otherwise, when you're done admiring your work, you can either:

  1. Go Back to the "Create Your Own Title" page and design a new title
  2. Print a sample version of what your title page may look like
  3. Start writing the story!
The page gives you options for all three of those possibilities by clicking on the relevant buttons.

How To Use the Main Character Generator Page

Enter the name of your main character.
Upload your character's profile image (Click on Choose File and navigate through your file system).
Enter the character's email address, height (in feet and then inches), backstory, birthday, most annoying habit, dominant personality trait, and catch-phrase.
You can leave out any of these last three characteristics if you so desire.
Click on NEXT

You will see a card displaying all the information about your character and the profile image.
If something went wrong, you will see the default profile image (the RN profile image).
NOTE: Something is likely to go wrong if you are not connected to a server or to localhost.
In that case, simply Go Back and try again!
Otherwise, when you're done admiring your work, you can either:
  1. Go Back to the "Create Your Own Main Character" page and design a new main character profile
  2. Print out the characterization of your main character
  3. Start writing the story!
The page gives you options for all three of those possibilities by clicking on the relevant buttons.

How To Use the Story Generator Page

There are three main parts to this page. The headlines dropdown, the page body text, and the "Does this story continue?" checkbox.
Before you write any story, the root headline is "Once Upon a Time." Every page of text is connected to a headline.
First, you fill the text for the first page of the story, and then you click the checkbox if this story branches out into two more options.
Click the Enter This Page button.
If you selected yes for "Does this story continue?" you will be prompted to fill in the headlines for the next two pages,
which will then appear in the headlines dropdown as the page reloads.
You select from the dropdown whichever direction of the story you want to work on and write the story for that page.
Just note that it does follow the order of the story - for example, you can't just skip all the way to the last page.
If you uncheck the "Does this story continue?" box, the words "THE END" will be added to the end of this page in your story
and you will be unable to continue that particular branch of your story.
There will be no headlines added to the dropdown list if the story doesn't continue.
If you finished the last page of the last branch of the story, you will be alerted that all stories have been finished.

At any point in the story or after you finish writing the story, you can either reset the story back to "Once Upon a Time",
Download a "stories.txt" file containing all the possible stories from start to finish written out in a text file
Or download a "story.txt" file containing a sample version of your story randomly placing your pages around the story while keeping track of the next page numbers.
And that's it! Have fun making your stories and combining all the components of your story (your title, main character profile, and actual story) into one beautiful creation written by you!
NOTE: Sample story is largely based off Giants, Elves, and Scary Monsters by R. G. Austin
