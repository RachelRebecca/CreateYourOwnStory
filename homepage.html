It all begins with your imagination and a "Once Upon a Time..."

This is the platform to can write your own "Choose Your Own Adventure" type story!

Coming up with a title page involves two things: An interesting name for your story as well as a gripping cover image. You can upload this image to our template title page designer and take a screenshot of your title page. We are currently designing this title page as well as capabilities to save a screenshot of your title page using the website.

Every "Choose Your Own Adventure" style story contains one important element: YOU! On this main character profile generator page you have the ability to create a profile and backstory for the main character in your story. The choices made by "you" throughout the story will be far more meaningful given a backstory and information of the significant milestones in your life. After all, a story about a 4-year-old who thinks there is a monster under his bed will be very different than an-eleven-year old whose trip on the bus to school takes a very strange turn, or a thirty-year-old who went on a cruise and was stranded on a desert island... The decision-making in this story begins with you designing that "you" that makes all the decisions in your stories.

The most important part of writing your own story is ... actually writing your own "Choose Your Own Adventure" style story! This page will actually help you break down your story in manageable parts using individual page headlines to keep you grounded even as the story veers down many different paths. On any given page, you can choose to either continue the story in two different directions, or make this page end its specific story, so that if the reader reaches this page, they've hit a "THE END" and must start over from the first page.

If at any point you have any confusion regarding the website, this manual is there to help! You can refer to this page at any point, even while actively writing the story, and it should offer a clear step-by-step outline of how to properly utilize this website as it provides unique capabilities in designing your very own "Choose Your Own Adventure" style story. Have fun getting creative!
